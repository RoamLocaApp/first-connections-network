First Connections is the umbrella platform uniting businesses, communities, and opportunities across the North East — through networking, awards, media, and regional projects.

Explore Our Network

First Connections is more than a networking brand — it's a regional movement built on connection, visibility, and opportunity. Darlington First and Newcastle First sit beneath it as local expressions of one joined-up North East campaign.
